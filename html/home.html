WordPress Social and Search Optimization

The Complete Structured Data Plugin for WordPress

Facebook / Open Graph Meta Tags, Google Merchant Feed XML, LinkedIn oEmbed, REST API, Pinterest Rich Pins, Google Rich Results, Image and Video SEO, Schema JSON-LD, Sitemaps XML, X (Twitter) Cards

WPSSO Core is a social and search optimization plugin to present your content at its best for social sites and search results, no matter how URLs are shared, reshared, messaged, posted, embedded, or crawled.
